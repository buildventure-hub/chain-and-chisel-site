FAQ

How much does a carving cost?

Pricing depends on size, wood type, and detail. Small yard pieces often begin around a few hundred. Medium pieces vary. Large custom commissions are quoted case-by-case. I’ll confirm pricing before carving begins.

How long does a commission take?

Typical turnaround is 2–4 weeks depending on size and current workload. I’ll confirm timeline before starting.

Do you deliver?

Pickup is available. Delivery can be arranged depending on size and distance.

How do I start?

Text a photo or description, size preference, and a budget range: (720) 334-6313

Do you do memorial carvings?

Yes — if you have reference photos and an idea of size/placement, I can quote it.
